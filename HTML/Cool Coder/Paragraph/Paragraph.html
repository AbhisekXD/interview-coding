About India

India, officially known as the Republic of India, is a country in South Asia. It is the seventh-largest country by land area, the second-most populous country, and the most populous democracy in the world. India is bounded by the Indian Ocean on the south, the Arabian Sea on the southwest, and the Bay of Bengal on the southeast. It shares land borders with Pakistan to the northwest, China and Nepal to the north, Bhutan to the northeast, and Bangladesh and Myanmar to the east. In the Indian Ocean, India is in the vicinity of Sri Lanka and the Maldives.

Home to ancient civilizations, India's history dates back thousands of years, with rich cultural and architectural heritage. It has been home to various dynasties, empires, and kingdoms, contributing significantly to global trade, culture, and science.

Geography

India's geography is diverse, featuring varied landscapes ranging from the Himalayas in the north to coastal plains and deserts in the west. The Ganges and the Brahmaputra rivers, among others, nourish fertile plains, while the Western and Eastern Ghats provide natural boundaries to the Deccan Plateau.
